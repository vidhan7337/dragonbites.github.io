About Us

Welcome to Dragon Bites – Where Every Bite Tells a Story!

At Dragon Bites, we bring the bold, irresistible flavors of Indo-Chinese cuisine straight to your plate. From sizzling starters to mouthwatering rice and noodle bowls, our dishes are crafted with fresh ingredients, authentic spices, and a whole lot of love.

Whether you're craving crispy Paneer, saucy Manchurian, or our signature Chinese platters, we've got something to delight every taste bud. We believe food should be a celebration — of taste, tradition, and togetherness.

The Founder - Jigar Shrimali

I’m Jigar Shrimali, the visionary behind Dragon Bites. Alongside this venture, I lead a real estate development firm, a full‑service marketing agency and a chain of nutrition stores—bringing strategic insight, creative energy and a passion for health to everything I do.
